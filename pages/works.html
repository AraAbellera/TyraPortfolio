OMORI Basil Animation



Some rigging/animation I did using After Effects. I have been using After Effects for around 2 years and these are some of the animations I have done. I recolor the needed areas on Ibispaint and mask the necessary parts using the puppet tool on After Effects. After breaking down all the parts , I compile them and rig them as to how I want it. I then add ssome graphs so that the animation is smoother and is not choppy,I am also able to make it faster or slower using this.
